📅 Monthly plan — 4 weeks

Each week has 21 meal slots (3 meals/day × 7). The bar shows how full and how varied each week is — aim for a mix of categories so no week feels repetitive. Use Balance settings to control repeat limits, prioritized tags, and cost/time preference.

🛒 Smart shopping list

Built from the whole month, scaled to your family, duplicates merged — then split by how often you buy: weekly items become one list per week, every-15-days items split into two stock-ups, and monthly items are one bulk run. Things that aren't in any recipe — salt, paper towels, cleaning supplies — can be added manually with "+ Add item"; they're tagged extra and can be removed anytime. Set ingredient prices on a recipe to see cost estimates here, and use Mark trip done to log actual spend on the Budget tab.
