MealMate - client file

Send MealMate-app.html to the buyer. If the platform blocks HTML files, send MealMate-client-app.zip instead.

How the buyer uses it:
1. Open MealMate-app.html in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or another browser.
2. Choose Arabic, French, or English in the first setup screen.
3. Their recipes, plan, shopping list, and budget are saved on their own device/browser.
4. They should use the Backup button inside the app to export a backup before changing device or clearing browser data.

No hosting or database is needed.
