📅 Monthly plan — 4 weeks

Each week has 21 meal slots (3 meals/day × 7). Tap Auto-balance the month to fill it with a varied mix, then tweak any day. Set the rules under Balance settings.

🛒 Smart shopping list

Built from the whole month, scaled to your family, duplicates merged — then split by how often you buy: weekly items become one list per week, every-15-days items split into two stock-ups, and monthly items are one bulk run. Things that aren't in any recipe — salt, paper towels, cleaning supplies — can be added manually with "+ Add item"; they're tagged extra and can be removed anytime. Type a price next to any item to see cost estimates, or tick what you bought and use Mark trip done to log the total you spent on the Budget tab.
